ABOUT-US
EDUAID

"EduAid is a web-based service that helps impoverished children with their education. EduAid promotes many types of assistance by linking volunteers and donors with poor children. Our platform takes use of cutting-edge web technologies to deliver an efficient and accessible user experience. To close the educational gap for poor children by utilizing technology and community resources. We hope to offer these youngsters with materials and opportunities that they wouldn't ordinarily get. We extensively assess the educational needs of each community we serve, ensuring that our programs meet their individual demands."

Our Service

Scholarships

Our service provides scholarships to support students in pursuing their educational goals by covering tuition and other related expenses. Scholarships are offered by Trust and Donors.

Financial support

Financial support provides funds to individuals to help cover expenses. This support can alleviate financial burdens and make essential opportunities more accessible.

Medical Support

Medical Support provides assistance to individuals needing help with medical expenses. It aims to ease the financial burden of healthcare costs, including treatments for children.

Education Support

Education Support provides resources and assistance to students, helping them achieve their academic goals. We provide tuition and camps for students each month.

Volunteership

Volunteership is the act of offering time and skills to help others or support a cause without financial compensation. Volunteers often find fulfillment in giving back and making a positive impact on society.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure assistance in maintaining, and improving essential physical and organizational structures. It ensures that facilities and resources are effectively managed to support various projects and operations.
