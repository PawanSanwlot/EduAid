Volunteers

Volunteers are the frontline workers in the website's mission to combat hunger and poverty. They actively seek out poor children or families in need, documenting their situations and collecting essential information.

Volunteers then post these details on their profiles or the trust's profile, effectively raising awareness of the needs within the community.

Their work involves not only identifying those in need but also accurately capturing and conveying their stories to ensure they receive appropriate help. Volunteers are crucial in bridging the gap between the needy and the trust, as their efforts initiate the process of assistance and support.
