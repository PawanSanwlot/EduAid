Trust

Trusts play a central role in the website's operations by overseeing and supporting the efforts initiated by volunteers. Upon receiving posts about poor children or families from volunteers, trusts verify the authenticity and urgency of the cases.

They then organize and coordinate the necessary support, often in collaboration with donors who provide financial assistance. Trusts ensure that the aid reaches the right beneficiaries efficiently and transparently.

Additionally, they manage resources, plan interventions, and monitor the impact of the support provided. Trusts act as an intermediary, connecting the compassionate efforts of volunteers with the generosity of donors, and ensuring that help is delivered effectively to those in need.
